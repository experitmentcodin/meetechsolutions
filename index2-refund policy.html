Refund Policy for Meetech Solutions

Effective Date: October 18, 2024

At Meetech Solutions, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with our services. However, if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are applicable under the following circumstances:

  • If the services provided are not delivered as promised or do not meet the agreed-upon specifications.
  • If you cancel your service request within 7 days of the initial payment and before the service delivery begins.

2. Non-Refundable Services

Certain services may be deemed non-refundable. This includes:

  • Services that have already been commenced or completed.
  • Customized services that have been tailored specifically for your needs.

3. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at the email or phone number provided below. Include the following information in your request:

  • Your name and contact information.
  • A detailed description of the reason for the refund request.
  • Any relevant documentation or evidence supporting your request.

4. Refund Process

Once we receive your refund request, we will:

  • Review the request and determine eligibility based on our policy.
  • Notify you of the decision within 14 business days.
  • If approved, process the refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Refund Policy on our website with an updated effective date.
